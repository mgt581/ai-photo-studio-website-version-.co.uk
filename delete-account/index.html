Account & Data Deletion – AI Photo Studio Pro

Users of AI Photo Studio Pro can delete their account and associated personal data directly within the app using the built-in account deletion feature.

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing:
support@aiphotostudio.co.uk

What data is deleted

  • User account information
  • Uploaded photos and images
  • Associated app usage data

Certain billing or transaction records may be retained where required by law.

All eligible data is deleted within 30 days.
